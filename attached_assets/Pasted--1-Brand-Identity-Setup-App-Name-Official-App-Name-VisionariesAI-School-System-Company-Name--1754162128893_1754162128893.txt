✅ 2. Splash Screen
⏳ Purpose:
Gives a branded introduction to users while the app is loading. Show the logo and name clearly.

📐 Layout:
Centered logo/icon (animated or static)

App Name below (bold, clean font)

Background color matching brand palette

Duration: ~2 seconds

🧩 Design Notes:
Use Lottie animation for extra flair (optional)

Example font: Poppins, Inter, or Nunito Sans

✅ 3. Onboarding Slides (After Splash)
🎯 Purpose:
Briefly introduce users to your app's core value and features before login.

✅ Screens (Recommended: 3 or 4 Slides)
Slide 1: Welcome
Title: Welcome to VisionariesAI School System

Subtitle: Empowering Schools with Smart Management

Image: Illustration of school/building with digital dashboards

Slide 2: Academic Excellence
Title: Manage Academics Easily

Subtitle: Timetables, Exams, Performance, and More

Image: Illustration of teacher and student with calendar and charts

Slide 3: All-in-One Administration
Title: Simplify School Operations

Subtitle: HR, Finance, Library, Hostel, Transport

Image: Admin dashboard illustration

(Optional) Slide 4: Stay Informed
Title: Real-time Updates and Notifications

Subtitle: Be notified instantly with smart alerts

Image: Bell icon with notification cards

📲 Navigation & Layout:
Swipeable slides using a swiper library like:

react-native-onboarding-swiper (easy)

react-native-snap-carousel (custom animations)

Dot indicators at the bottom

Final screen: "Get Started" button → navigates to login/signup

Top-right: Skip button for quicker entry

🧠 UX Guidance:
Keep slides minimal and engaging (less than 20 words per slide)

Use illustrative graphics, not heavy text

Ensure theme matches app (light/dark versions)
